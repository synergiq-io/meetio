## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### **Problem: Upload fails**
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (<50MB total)
- Use PNG or JPG format only
- Ensure files are images, not PDFs
- Try uploading fewer slides at once

### **Problem: Slides out of order**
**Solutions:**
- Name files: Slide01, Slide02, Slide03...
- Select files in correct order when uploading
- Use leading zeros (01, 02 not 1, 2)

### **Problem: Participants can't see slides**
**Solutions:**
- Check their internet connection
- Ask them to refresh browser
- Verify presentation actually started
- Check they're in the same meeting room

### **Problem: Slides not updating**
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Click next/previous again
- Close and restart presentation
- Refresh and rejoin meeting
